Twenty seventeen

Thankful for another year

Traveling on my own was incredibly rewarding, it helped me to see the possibilities of what I could be. Visiting Marseille and Paris (my only two trips in a few years now) hit that home for me.

The last three months of 2017 have been encouraging. I have stood up for myself a few times and I would like to always keep this as a guiding principle for the rest of my life. Standing up for oneself isn't limited to defending one's honour in front of others, it is about remaining true to yourself despite the challenging and weak moments, no matter how micro or macro they may be. For myself, it's about remaining steadfast and trusting in my needs and wants, ideas, and in my projects. And with that, I am walking into this new year aware of the challenges that await me, aware of the strength and the will within me to make it.
